How It Works

  
Complete ride flow — from booking to payment

  

    

      
👤 Rider Experience

      
1
Open app → GPS auto-detects location in Taliparamba

      
2
Search destination → Google Places autocomplete with local landmarks

      
3
See fare → Transparent upfront pricing based on real road distance

      
4
Book auto → Smart matching finds nearest available driver

      
5
Track live → Real-time driver location on map via Socket.io

      
6
Verify OTP → 4-digit OTP shared with driver to start ride

      
7
Pay & rate → Cash or digital payment, rate the driver

    

    

      
🛺 Driver Experience

      
1
Go online → Location tracked via GPS, appears on rider searches

      
2
Get request → Ride alert with pickup, drop, fare, distance

      
3
Accept/decline → 15-second timer, no penalty for occasional declines

      
4
Navigate → Turn-by-turn directions to pickup via Google Maps

      
5
Verify OTP → Enter rider's OTP to confirm correct passenger

      
6
Drive → Live navigation to drop-off with real route polyline

      
7
Earn 100% → Zero commission, full fare goes to driver
